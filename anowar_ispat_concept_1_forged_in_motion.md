# ANOWAR ISPAT – WEBSITE DEMO CONCEPT
## Concept 1: FORGED IN MOTION
### Theme: From Raw Fire to Engineered Perfection

---

## 1. CORE IDEA
A cinematic storytelling experience where steel is "born from fire" and transforms into the infrastructure that builds Bangladesh. The entire website scroll acts as a visual journey of power, precision, and innovation.

Emotional keywords:
- Strength
- Transformation
- National impact
- Technological superiority

Brand positioning:
**“The force that builds the nation.”**

---

## 2. VISUAL DIRECTION

### Color Palette
- Primary: Matte black (#0B0B0B)
- Secondary: Gunmetal (#2A2A2A)
- Accent: Molten orange (#FF6A00)
- Highlight: Chrome / silver gradients

### Typography
- Headline: Bold, wide, industrial sans-serif
- Body: Clean modern grotesk
- Massive kinetic typography for section transitions

### Design Language
- Liquid metal surfaces
- Heat glow & spark particles
- Glass + chrome UI panels
- Industrial lighting and shadows

---

## 3. HERO SECTION EXPERIENCE

### Layout
Fullscreen immersive 3D environment.

### Animation Flow
1. Dark screen with distant furnace sound
2. Molten steel appears
3. Sparks reacting to cursor movement
4. Scroll → molten metal flows and forms a perfect steel rod
5. Headline fades in:
   **FORGED IN FIRE. ENGINEERED FOR THE FUTURE.**

### Interactions
- Mouse movement → liquid distortion
- Magnetic CTA button
- Heat glow intensity reacts to scroll speed

---

## 4. SCROLL STORY STRUCTURE

### SECTION 1 – RAW ENERGY
Visual: Blast furnace environment
Effects:

- Depth-based parallax
- Heatwave distortion

About the company’s manufacturing power & capacity.

## 6. TECHNOLOGY STACK
For pitch presentation:

- Three.js → 3D steel & molten simulation
- GSAP + ScrollTrigger → cinematic scroll control
- Lenis / Locomotive → smooth scrolling
- WebGL shaders → heat distortion & reflections
- Blender → high-quality product models
